You have read the Why. You have read the What. This is the How — the engine beneath the architecture.

Portico is not a dashboard. It is not a tracker. It is not an aggregator of devices. It is a proactive, closed-loop intelligence system with six specialized agents, each focused on a single domain of your biological and financial life — and one orchestrating intelligence that holds them all together.

What follows is the operating model: how each agent thinks, what it measures, what it does, and how the system moves as one.

The Orchestration Layer

Portico moves like
a circadian clock.

The body doesn't treat all its systems equally at all times. Neither does Portico.

05:00 — 10:00

Morning

Sage — metabolic priming
Apex — exercise timing
Dusk — morning light protocol

Cortisol peaks. Insulin sensitivity is highest. Exercise and food timing now compound forward into the rest of the day.

10:00 — 17:00

Midday

Sage — food timing & patterns
Still — awe, breath, connection
Grove — capital decisions

Cognitive performance peaks mid-morning. Emotional regulation is strongest. Grove surfaces financial decisions here, not at midnight.

17:00 — 21:00

Evening

Dusk — wind-down initiation
Sage — evening food boundaries
Still — meditation prompt

Melatonin begins to rise. Dusk takes the lead. Light exposure now shapes what happens in four hours — and the quality of tomorrow morning.

21:00 — 05:00

Night

Dusk — sleep architecture
Portico — overnight synthesis

The system integrates. While you sleep, Portico computes the day's cross-domain patterns and prepares the morning brief. Recovery is not passive — it is the compounding.

Morning light affects tonight's sleep. Food timing through the day affects tomorrow's metabolic state. Exercise timing affects recovery and sleep architecture. Stress from a financial decision at midnight impairs cortisol rhythm the next morning.

Portico holds all of these temporal connections simultaneously — not as a feature, but as the foundation of how the system thinks. This is what separates an operating system from a collection of apps.

Sleep
Dusk
Foundation
Mitigates: Metabolic Disease

Sleep happens to you. That is the point. Dusk does not try to force sleep — it creates the conditions in which sleep can occur. Its model is CBT-I: Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for Insomnia, the most clinically effective sleep intervention. Three levers: wind-down, light, and timing. When a night goes badly, Dusk encourages. It does not catastrophize.

Movement · Exercise
Apex
Right Structural Pillar
Mitigates: Cancer

The aerobic triangle: Zone 2 base and VO₂ max peak define two sides. The goal is to maximize the area between them. Strength is the third pillar. Balance is the fourth. Apex gives you the framework and personalized protocols — not micromanaged execution details. It won't tell you to run at 5 miles per hour. It will explain why your aerobic base is the foundation of everything else.

Mind · Cognition
Still
The Roof
Mitigates: Neurodegeneration

Still operates with deliberate humility. Three entry points, minimum effective dose. Meditation — five minutes of breath regulation has measurable physiological effect. Awe — going outside and encountering something larger than yourself is one of the fastest cortisol reducers known. Connection — social contact is biological protection. Still explains what happens in your body when you do each of these. It illuminates. It does not prescribe.

The System in Motion

No domain operates alone.

This is not a feature. It is a design principle. The domains amplify each other — in both directions.

→

→

→

→

→
Better decisions improve capital allocation and compound Grove's flywheel

Optionality. The ability to choose your work. The ability to absorb shocks without collapse. The ability to redirect time. This is the ultimate output of compounded biological and financial capital — and it is what Portico exists to build.
